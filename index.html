What is New Moon Library?

It's a list of idle books available in town. Books are added to this website and people can request them via email or contact page.

How does it work?

People who have books that they don't like or need anymmore, and what them available for the community can contact this website mentioning the details about their books and their location. Then these books will be added, and people interested in them can request them online.

Website's purposes & goals

I started this website as a personal project to learn HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Design. As my knowledge improve, more features will be added. I thought it would be nice to build this project on something useful and nice: a library.
